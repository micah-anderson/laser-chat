Step into a world of transformation alongside orphaned youth through YSA Outreach Adventures. Our tailored expeditions go beyond the ordinary, offering extraordinary experiences that elevate spirits and unveil the boundless possibilities life holds. Join us in empowering these resilient young souls by embarking on unforgettable trips and adventures that redefine their perspectives.

Our journeys take us through some of the most breathtaking landscapes, beginning with British Columbia's enchanting West Coast Trail. Trek through dense forests, traverse rugged terrains, and witness nature's grandeur as we encourage and uplift these remarkable youth. The adventure continues as we venture to the majestic Yukon, reaching all the way up to the awe-inspiring King's Throne. Here, we inspire the youth to conquer challenges, teaching them the rewards of determination and perseverance.

Exploration takes us to the heart of the Great Bear Rainforest, where we immerse ourselves in one of Earth's most pristine ecosystems. Amidst this natural wonder, our young adventurers discover the importance of conservation and their role in safeguarding our planet's treasures.

Throughout this incredible journey, we embark on horseback riding expeditions, casting lines for fishing escapades, and navigating the serene waters through canoeing and kayaking. As evening descends, we gather around the campfire under some of the clearest skies on Earth, fostering a space for learning, sharing stories, and building connections that will span a lifetime.

Our adventures commence and culminate at the YSA Headquarters in Nemiah Valley, a place of cultural enrichment, teamwork, and camaraderie. Here, we delve into the essence of community and culture, discovering the strength that emerges when we unite. Friendships forged during these expeditions are more than companionships; they are bonds that endure.

Join us as we weave tales of courage and resilience, painting a future of endless promise for these orphaned youth. Through hiking, exploration, horseback riding, fishing, and fireside learning, YSA Outreach Adventures empower young hearts to discover the world's magnificence while discovering the beauty within themselves.

Together We Face Global Challenges.

In a world shaped by complex issues, YSA stands at the forefront of change.

Pollution, habitat loss, development, and climate change cast shadows on our environment, demanding our attention and action.

The time to make a difference is now.

Here are just a few compelling realities:

  • By 2050, air pollution could be responsible for 9 million premature deaths annually.
  • Water scarcity affects over 2 billion people, a crisis exacerbated by pollution and mismanagement.
  • Food security faces challenges from climate change, impacting agricultural systems and supply chains.
  • Shelter and housing are basic rights, yet millions lack safe and adequate living conditions.
  • Clothing waste contributes to environmental degradation, with the fashion industry's impact growing.
  • Deforestation continues at an alarming rate, threatening ecosystems and exacerbating climate change.
  • The creation and preservation of wildlife habitats is crucial to maintaining biodiversity and balance.
  • World peace remains an aspiration, as conflicts disrupt lives and nations across the globe.

YSA envisions a world where our actions reverberate positively across every corner of the Earth.
